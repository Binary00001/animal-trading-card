AXOLOTL

Axolotl have the ability to regenerate body organs and lost limbs up to 5 times!

  • Scientific Name: Ambystoma mexicanum
  • Size: 15 - 45 cm (6 - 18 in)
  • Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: Freshwater lakes & ponds

A neotenic salamander commonly referred to as the 'Mexican Walking Fish', though not actually a fish, which can be found in freshwater lakes and ponds in Mexico.
